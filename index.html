About us

Magic Kingdom is located at the Walt Disney World Resort in Lake Buena Vista, Florida, just outside of Orlando. It is my favorite park because it is where all my favorite movies and shows come to life. Whether it's seeing the iconic Cinderella Castle or racing through the stars on Space Mountian, the park is filled with the kind of magic and wonder that makes it the "Most Magical Place on Earth."
